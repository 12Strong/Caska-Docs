---
title: Managing Fundraisers
description: Track fundraising campaigns in Caska — log paper and online sales, calculate earnings owed to the organization, and maintain a historical archive.
---

The Fundraisers module lets you track fundraising campaigns — typically school or community fundraisers where your products are sold on behalf of an organization.

## Creating a fundraiser

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Fundraisers and click New Fundraiser" titleType="p">
    Click **Fundraisers** in the main navigation, then **New Fundraiser**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in the campaign details" titleType="p">
    - **Fundraiser name** *(required)*
    - Linked customer (the organization running the fundraiser)
    - Start and end dates
    - Sales channels (paper order forms, online, etc.)
    - Commission/earnings percentage
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save" titleType="p">
    Click **Save** to create the campaign record.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tracking sales

As the fundraiser runs, update the sales figures on the fundraiser record:

- **Paper sales** — revenue collected from physical order forms
- **Online sales** — revenue from online orders
- **Expenses** — shipping costs and other costs

## How earnings are calculated

Caska automatically calculates two figures:

- **Earnings paid** — the amount you owe the fundraising organization: `Total Revenue − Paper Sales − Shipping Cost`
- **Net revenue** — your take after all costs (display only, not editable)

<Callout kind="info">
  These fields are calculated automatically — you don't enter them manually. Just keep your sales and expense figures up to date.
</Callout>

## Archiving a fundraiser

When a campaign is complete, archive it to move it out of your active list. Go to the **Archive** tab to view all historical fundraiser records.
