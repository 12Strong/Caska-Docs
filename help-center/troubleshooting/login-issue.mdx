---
title: Can't log in to Caska
description: Resolve login problems quickly.
---

## Symptoms

- Login page reloads without error
- Forgot password link not working

## Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    ````
    1. Go to https://caska.com/login
    2. Click "Forgot password?"
    3. Check your email for reset link
    4. Follow instructions to set new password
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Still no access?">
    ````
    Reach out to support@caska.com with your account email and error details.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
