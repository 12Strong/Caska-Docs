## Overview

Caska organizes your food manufacturing docs.

## Steps

1. Visit `https://caska.com/signup`
2. Create an account with your email
3. Confirm your email address
4. Log in to Caska dashboard
5. Invite team members (optional)
6. Start adding your first project or recipe docs

## Quick Tip

<Callout kind="info">
You can import existing docs from CSV or PDF in the Projects menu.
</Callout>
