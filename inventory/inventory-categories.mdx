---
title: Inventory Categories
description: Organize your raw materials and supplies using categories like Dry Ingredients, Packaging, or Flavourings for easier filtering and management.
---

Categories let you organize your raw materials by type — for example, Dry Ingredients, Packaging, Flavourings, or Oils.

## Setting up categories

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Inventory" titleType="p">
    Find the Inventory section in your account settings.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a category" titleType="p">
    Under **Categories**, click **Add Category**, enter a name, and save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Assigning categories

When creating or editing an inventory item, select a category from the dropdown.

## Filtering by category

Use the column controls on the Inventory list to filter down to a specific category.

<Callout kind="info">
  Inventory categories are configured separately from Product and Customer
  categories.
</Callout>
