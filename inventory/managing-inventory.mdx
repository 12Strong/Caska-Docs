The Inventory module tracks your raw materials and packaging supplies — quantities on hand, costs, supplier information, and stock movements.

## Adding an inventory item

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Inventory and click New Item" titleType="p">
    Click **Inventory** in the main navigation, then **+ New**.
  </Step>

<Step title="Fill in the details" titleType="p">
  - **Item name** *(required)* - Category - Weight/Volume/Length, etc per unit -
  Unit of measure (kg, L, each, etc.) - Unit cost - Supplier name and contact
  info - Current stock quantity - Low stock alert threshold
</Step>

  <Step title="Save" titleType="p">
    Click **Save** to create the record.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Entering inventory purchase units

Caska is smart enough to navigate the difference between a purchased inventory unit and the unit of measurement for said unit. If you purchase bags of sugar at 4kg per bag, you'll enter 4 and kg in the weight per unit fields and "bag" in the purchase unit name. When restocking, you'll enter the number of bags you restocked (and not the weight of said bags.)

## Editing an inventory item

Click any item in the list to open its detail page. You can update any field and view the full stock movement history from here.

## Stock movements

Inventory stock changes in two ways:

- **Restocks** — manually log new stock when you receive a delivery. Open the item and use the **Restock** action or click the add stock button on the main inventory page.
- **Order fulfillment** — when an order is created, Caska automatically decrements the linked raw material quantities based on the product's recipe.

## Linking inventory to products

To connect an inventory item to a product's recipe, open the product and add the item as a recipe ingredient or packaging material. See [Recipe Ingredients & Costing](/products/recipe-costing) for details.
