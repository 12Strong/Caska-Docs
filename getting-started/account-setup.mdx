---
title: Setting Up Your Account
description: Configure your Caska account with company details, unit preferences, timezone, and key settings that apply across every module.
---

When you first sign in to Caska, a short onboarding flow walks you through the core configuration. These settings apply account-wide, so it's worth getting them right before you start adding data.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your company information" titleType="p">
    Provide your business name and basic details. This appears on orders and exported documents.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your plan" titleType="p">
    Select the plan that fits your current needs. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time from **Settings → Billing**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure company settings" titleType="p">
    These preferences apply across every module:

    - **Unit system** — Imperial or metric. Affects how weights and measurements display throughout the app.
    - **Date format** — Choose your preferred date display format.
    - **Timezone** — Used for scheduled notifications and report date groupings.
    - **Week starts on** — Sunday or Monday. Affects timesheet pay period calculations.

    <Callout kind="alert">
      Set your timezone accurately. Notification alerts fire at 8 AM in your configured timezone — a wrong setting means alerts arrive at the wrong time.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify your email" titleType="p">
    Check your inbox and click the verification link. Caska requires a verified email before you can log in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After setup

Once you're in, a few things worth doing right away:

- **Invite your team** — Go to **Settings → Team** to add employees or admins. See [Inviting Your Team](/getting-started/inviting-your-team).
- **Add your first customer** — Head to **Customers** and create your first account.
- **Set up your product catalogue** — Go to **Products** and add your products, including pricing and recipe details.
- **Configure notifications** — Go to **Settings → Notifications** to set up low stock alerts and timesheet reminders.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Need to change any of these settings later? Go to **Settings → Account** at any time.
</Callout>
