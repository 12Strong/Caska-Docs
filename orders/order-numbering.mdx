---
title: Order Auto-Numbering
description: Configure Caska's sequential order numbering to match your existing system, including custom prefixes and starting numbers.
---

Caska assigns a sequential number to each new order automatically. You can configure the format to match your existing numbering convention.

## Configuring order numbering

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Orders" titleType="p">
    Find the Orders section in your account settings.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set your prefix and starting number" titleType="p">
    Under **Order Numbering**:

    - **Prefix** — text that appears before the number (e.g. `ORD-`, `SO-`, or your company initials)
    - **Starting number** — where numbering begins (useful when migrating from another system)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save" titleType="p">
    Numbers increment automatically with each new order from this point.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  If you're migrating from another system, set the starting number above your highest existing order number to avoid duplicates.
</Callout>

You cannot manually set an order number on individual orders — numbering is always sequential.
