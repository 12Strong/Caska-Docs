---
title: Custom Fields for Orders
description: Add custom fields to orders to capture order-specific information like PO numbers, delivery instructions, or sales rep names.
---

Custom fields let you capture order-specific information that isn't in Caska's standard order form — for example, purchase order numbers, delivery instructions, or sales rep names.

## Setting up order custom fields

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Orders" titleType="p">
    Find the Orders section in your account settings.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a custom field" titleType="p">
    Under **Custom Fields**, click **Add Custom Field**, enter a label, and save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The field appears on every order form going forward.

## Using custom fields on orders

When creating or editing an order, scroll to the Custom Fields section at the bottom of the form. Fill in the values and save as normal.

## Showing custom fields in the order list

Use the **Columns** button on the Orders list to toggle custom field column visibility.

<Callout kind="info">
  Custom fields work the same way across all modules. See [Custom Fields](/customers/custom-fields) for the full overview.
</Callout>
