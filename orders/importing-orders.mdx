---
title: Importing Orders
description: Import historical order data into Caska via CSV when migrating from a spreadsheet or another system.
---

You can import historical order data into Caska using a CSV file — useful when migrating from a spreadsheet or another system.

## Before you import

Format your CSV with these columns:

- **Customer Name** _(required)_
- Order Number
- Due Date
- Status
- Product Name, Quantity, Unit Price _(one row per line item — refer to the sample template for the exact format)_
- Notes

<Callout kind="tip">
  Download the **sample template** from the Import dialog before building your
  CSV — it shows the exact column names and expected format.
</Callout>

## How to import

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Data" titleType="p">
    Click on the Orders tab to load the page. Optionally, download the CSV template to get started.
  </Step>

<Step title="Upload your CSV file" titleType="p">
  Select and upload your file.
</Step>

<Step title="Review the import summary" titleType="p">
  Check how many records were imported and review any skipped rows.
</Step>

</Steps>

## After importing

Review the imported orders in your list.

<Callout kind="info">
  Imported orders match customers by name. If the customer name exactly matches
  an existing record, they'll be linked. If not, you may need to manually update
  the customer association on those orders.
</Callout>
