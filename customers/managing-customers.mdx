---
title: Managing Customers
description: Add, edit, and organize your customer accounts in Caska, including contact details, addresses, discounts, and engagement tracking.
---

The Customers module is your account directory. Each customer record holds contact details, billing and shipping addresses, engagement status, order history, and any custom fields you've set up.

## Adding a customer

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Customers" titleType="p">
    Click **Customers** in the main navigation.
  </Step>

<Step title="Click +New" titleType="p">
  This opens the customer creation form.
</Step>

<Step title="Fill in the customer details" titleType="p">
  - **Company name** *(required)* - Parent Company (this will append to a
  customer's name eg. Parent Company - Customer Name) - Contact name, email,
  phone - Billing and shipping addresses - Discount (applied automatically to
  orders for this customer) - Terms - Checkbox, Wether to send follow-up emails
  - Checkbox, if the customer is active
</Step>

  <Step title="Save" titleType="p">
    Click **Save** to create the record.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing a customer

Click any customer in the list to open their detail page. From there you can edit any field and view their full order history.

## Searching and sorting

Use the search bar at the top of the list to find by name or contact info. Sort by engagement status or any other column using the column controls.

## Deleting a customer

Open a customer's record and use the delete option at the bottom of the edit form.

<Callout kind="info">
  Deleting a customer does not remove their historical orders. Customer names
  are stored on order records at the time of creation, so order history is
  preserved.
</Callout>

## Parent companies

If a customer belongs to a larger organization, you can set a **Parent Company**. This links the customer to a parent account and is visible in order records and dropdowns.
