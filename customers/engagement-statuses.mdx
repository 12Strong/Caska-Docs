---
title: Engagement Statuses
description: Track the health of each customer relationship with customizable engagement statuses, and connect them to Caska's automated follow-up system.
---

Engagement statuses let you track where each customer stands in your relationship — whether they're active, at risk, lapsed, or anywhere in between. You define the statuses and what they mean for your business.

## Setting up statuses

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Customers" titleType="p">
    Find the Customers section in your account settings.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a status" titleType="p">
    Under **Engagement Statuses**, click **Add Status**. Give it a name (e.g. Active, At Risk, Lapsed, New Lead) and choose a color.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save" titleType="p">
    The status is now available in the customer form and visible as a color-coded indicator in the customer list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can create as many statuses as you need.

## Assigning a status to a customer

When creating or editing a customer, select their current engagement status from the dropdown. You can update this at any time.

## Connection to Follow-Up

Caska's Follow-Up system uses engagement data to trigger automated re-engagement emails to customers who haven't ordered in a while. See [How Follow-Up Works](/follow-up/how-follow-up-works) for full details.

## Dashboard snapshot

Your Dashboard shows a weekly summary of customer counts by engagement status — useful for spotting shifts in your account health at a glance.
