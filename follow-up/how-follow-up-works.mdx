---
title: How the Follow-Up System Works
description: Automate re-engagement outreach to customers who haven't ordered recently using Caska's built-in follow-up email sequences.
---

The Follow-Up module automates outreach to customers who haven't ordered in a while. It sends a sequence of reminder emails on a schedule you define — so staying in front of your accounts doesn't rely on memory.

## The concept

Each customer has a **follow-up date** — calculated from their last order. When that date passes, Caska queues a follow-up email and continues sending on your defined schedule until the customer re-orders or they exit the sequence.

## Setting up the system

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Communication" titleType="p">
    Find the Communication section in your account settings.
  </Step>

<Step title="Configure re-engagement settings" titleType="p">
  - **Days since last order** before the first follow-up triggers - **Days
  between follow-up emails** — how many days to wait in between emails - **Max
  follow-ups per customer** - hoe many emails to send in total
</Step>

    <Step title="Configure your sending details" titleType="p">
    Choose how many emails in total to send each day and what time of day to send them.

  </Step>

<Step title="Set up the email settings - Caska or SMTP" titleType="p">
  Choose whether Caska will send the emails on your behalf (has a higher chance
  of being seen as spam) or connect to an email address you own and Caska will
  use it to send (comes directly from you.) If sending from an email address you
  own, enter the connection details.
</Step>

<Step title="Create your email signature" titleType="p">
  Design your email signature to match what you use in your regular email.
</Step>

  <Step title="Save" titleType="p">
    The system activates and begins monitoring customer order dates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Creating the Follow-up Email Messages

Go to **Settings → Communication → Follow-up Emails** to create sets of subject lines and email bodys to send to your customers. Caska will randomly choose one subject line and one email body to send, making sure that the messages mimic being sent directly from you.

Click on **+ADD SUBJECT** to add subject lines or click on the trash icon to remove existing options.

Click on **+ADD BODY** to add email body copy or click on the trash icon to remove existing options.

You can use {{customer_name}} and {{company_name}} as personalisation tokens in your messages, but use sparingly as there are no fallback values for these.

Click on **Save Templates** when you are done.

## The Follow-Up dashboard

The **Follow-Up** section in the navigation (Owners and Admins only) shows:

- Customers currently in the re-engagement queue
- Which follow-up number each customer is on
- When their next email is scheduled

## Opting customers out

Exclude a customer from follow-up emails by updating their follow up status in their customer details page.

<Callout kind="info">
  Follow-up emails should be configured to use your SMTP email settings. This
  will ensure that they are alwasy delivered. Emails sent by Caska's own email
  system have a much higher chance of being sent to a spam or junk folder.
</Callout>
