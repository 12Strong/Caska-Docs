title: Billing & Subscription
description: Manage your Caska plan, update your payment method, understand plan changes, and learn about founding member pricing.
---

## Viewing your current plan

Go to **Settings → Billing** to see your current plan, next billing date, and payment method on file.

## Changing your plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Billing" titleType="p">
    Find the Billing section in your account settings.
  </Step>

<Step title="Click Change Plan" titleType="p">
  Review the available plans.
</Step>

  <Step title="Select and confirm" titleType="p">
    Choose the plan you want and confirm the change.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Plan changes take effect immediately. Upgrades are charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the current billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current period.

## Updating your payment method

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Billing" titleType="p">
    Find the Billing section in your account settings and click on the Card tab.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Update Card Details" titleType="p">
    Enter your new card details and save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Cancelling your subscription

Go to **Settings → Billing** and select the cancellation option. Your account stays active until the end of your current billing period. After that, your data is retained for a 30 day grace period before deletion.

<Callout kind="tip">
  If you're considering cancelling due to a specific issue, reach out to support
  first — we may be able to help.
</Callout>

## Founding member pricing

If you joined during the founding member period, your discounted rate is locked in for life as long as your subscription stays active.

<Callout kind="alert">
  Cancelling and resubscribing will lose any special promotional rates you
  currently have on your plan. It cannot be reinstated.
</Callout>
