## Changing your password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Account → Password" titleType="p">
    Find the Password section under Account settings.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your current and new password" titleType="p">
    Enter your current password, then enter and confirm your new one.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save" titleType="p">
    Your password is updated immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If you've forgotten your password, use the **Forgot Password** link on the login screen to receive a reset email.

## Two-factor authentication (2FA)

Two-factor authentication requires a verification code in addition to your password when signing in from a new device.

### Enabling 2FA

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Account → Security" titleType="p">
    Find the Security section under Account settings.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Enable Two-Factor Authentication" titleType="p">
    You'll need an authenticator app on your phone (Google Authenticator, Authy, or similar).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Scan the QR code and verify" titleType="p">
    Scan with your authenticator app and enter the verification code to confirm setup.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save your backup codes" titleType="p">
    Store these in a secure location. They're your recovery option if you lose access to your authenticator app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Disabling 2FA

Go to **Settings → Account → Security** and click **Disable Two-Factor Authentication**. You'll need to verify with your current 2FA code to confirm.

## Suspicious sign-in detection

Caska monitors for unusual login activity. If a sign-in looks suspicious (new device, new location, new browser simultaneously), you may be asked to verify via a magic link sent to your email.

<Callout kind="info">
  This is normal behavior on first login from a new browser or device. The magic link email arrives within a few minutes.
</Callout>

## API Keys

If your plan includes API access, manage API keys under **Settings → API Keys**. API access is available to users with Developer permissions — contact your account Owner to enable it.
