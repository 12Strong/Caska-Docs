The Reports section gives you visibility into your business performance across sales, production, inventory, and customers — all in one place.

Reports is available to **Owners and Admins**. Employees do not have access.

## Report types

Caska includes reports across several areas:

- **Sales** — order volume and revenue over time
- **Products** — top-selling products, revenue by product
- **Inventory** — stock usage and restock history
- **Customers** — order frequency and engagement trends
- **Batches** — production volume over time _(if Batches is enabled on your account)_
- **Timesheets** — hours by employee and pay period

## Date filters

Every report includes date range controls:

- **Preset ranges** — quick options for the last 30, 90, 180, or 365 days
- **Custom range** — select a specific From and To date
- **Period grouping** — toggle between Day, Week, or Month to control how data is grouped on charts

<Callout kind="info">
  Reports group orders by **due date**, not creation date. This means imported
  historical orders appear in their correct time periods.
</Callout>

## Exporting report data

Each report includes a data table below the chart. Click **Export CSV** to download the raw data for use in a spreadsheet or external reporting tool.
