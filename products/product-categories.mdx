---
title: Product Categories
description: Organize your product catalogue using categories by product type, flavour line, dietary designation, or any grouping that makes sense for your business.
---

Product categories help you organize your catalogue — by product type, flavour line, dietary designation, or however your range is structured.

## Setting up categories

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Products → Categories" titleType="p">
    Find the Products section in your account settings and click on Categories.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a category" titleType="p">
    Under **Categories**, click **Add Category**, enter a name, and save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Assigning a category

When creating or editing a product, select a category from the dropdown. A product can belong to one category.

## Filtering by category

On the Products list, use the column controls or filter options to view products in a specific category.

<Callout kind="info">
  Product categories are separate from Inventory and Customer categories — each
  is configured under its own Settings section.
</Callout>
