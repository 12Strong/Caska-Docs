title: Tracking Product Stock
description: Understand how Caska tracks finished product stock levels, logs every movement, and alerts you when stock runs low.
---

Caska tracks finished product stock levels and logs every movement — so you always know how much you have on hand and why it changed.

## How stock is tracked

Product stock changes in two ways:

- **Order fulfillment** — stock is decremented automatically when an order moves to a completed status
- **Manual adjustments** — you can add or subtract stock directly from a product's detail page, useful for reconciling after a physical count or correcting errors

## Viewing stock history

On any product's detail page, scroll to the **Stock History** section to see a log of all stock movements with dates, quantities, and references.

## Setting up low stock alerts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Notifications" titleType="p">
    Find the Notifications section in your account settings.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure product stock alerts" titleType="p">
    Under **Product Stock**, set the low stock threshold and notification frequency (daily, weekly, or on first drop).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save" titleType="p">
    You'll receive an email alert when any product falls below that level.
  </Step>
</Steps>

See [Notification Alerts](/settings/notifications) for all configuration options.

## Stock and batch production

If you use the Batches module, completing a batch can update the corresponding product stock to reflect new finished goods. See [Managing Batches](/features/batches/managing-batches) for details.
