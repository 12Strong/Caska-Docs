---
title: Managing Products
description: Add and manage your product catalogue in Caska, including pricing, recipe ingredients, stock level (if batch-create) and profit margins.
---

The Products module is your product catalogue. Each record holds pricing, costing, stock level (if batch-create), recipe, and any custom fields you've set up.

## Adding a product

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Products and click New Product" titleType="p">
    Click **Products** in the main navigation, then **+ New**.
  </Step>

<Step title="Fill in the product details" titleType="p">
  - **Product name** *(required)* - Category - SKU, GTIN, public link - Price
  and unit - Cost per sellable unit *(calculated automatically from recipe, or
  enter manually)*
</Step>

<Step title="Add a product image and barcode" titleType="p">
  Optional but recommended if you want visual identification in lists and forms.
</Step>

<step title="Complete the product costing calculator fields" titleType="p">
  Choose your inventory items for both the sellable unit and the recipe batch
  for your product, entering in the amounts of each inventory item used.
  Indicate how many individual units a recipe creates and if enabled, how many
  hours of labour are required.
</step>

  <Step title="Save" titleType="p">
    Click **Save** to create the product.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing a product

Click any product in the list to open its detail page. You can edit all fields and view stock movement history from here if your account is set to batch-create.

## Activating and deactivating products

Products can be marked as **active** or **inactive**. Inactive products are hidden from order forms but their records are preserved — useful for seasonal items or discontinued products.

## Product sort order

Products follow a custom sort order, not alphabetical. Drag and drop rows in the product list to reorder. The order you set here is the order products appear in order forms and dropdowns throughout the app.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Set your sort order intentionally — your best-sellers or most-used products at
  the top saves time when creating orders.
</Callout>
