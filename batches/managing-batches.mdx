---
title: Managing Batches
description: Track production runs in Caska — create batches linked to products, move them through statuses, and maintain a full archive of completed production history.
---

The Batches module tracks your production runs. Each batch is tied to a specific product and records how many recipe units you produced, the run status, and any custom information you track.

<Callout kind="info">
  Batches is an account-level feature. If you don't see it in your navigation,
  enable it in Settings → Account.
</Callout>

## Creating a batch

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Batches and click New Batch" titleType="p">
    Click **Batches** in the main navigation, then **+ New**.
  </Step>

<Step title="Select the product you're producing" titleType="p">
  Choose from your active product catalogue.
</Step>

<Step title="Enter production details" titleType="p">
  - Number of recipes (production quantity) - Batch date - Any notes
</Step>

  <Step title="Save" titleType="p">
    Caska assigns a sequential batch number automatically (e.g. B-0001).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Batch statuses

When a batch is completed, Caska will update the corresponding product stock to reflect the new finished goods. The completed batch will show on the archived page.

## Archiving batches

Completed batches can be archived to keep your active list clean. Go to the **Archive** tab to view all completed production history or to reactivate a batch and move it to the active list.

## Custom fields

Add custom fields to batches under **Settings → Custom Fields** — useful for tracking equipment used, yield notes, QC results, or any other production-specific data.
