---
title: Custom Fields
description: Add custom data fields to capture business-specific information that isn't covered by Caska's standard fields — available across Customers, Products, Inventory, Orders, Batches, and Timesheets.
---

Custom fields let you capture information specific to your business that Caska's standard fields don't cover — things like account managers, territories, contract expiry dates, or internal codes.

Custom fields are available on: **Customers, Products, Inventory, Orders, Batches, and Timesheets**.

## Setting up custom fields

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Custom Fields" titleType="p">
    Each module has its own custom field configuration section.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a custom field" titleType="p">
    Under **Custom Fields**, click **Add Custom Field**. Give it a label (e.g. "Account Manager", "Territory", "Contract Expiry") and save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once added, the field appears on every record form for that module.

## Filling in custom field values

When creating or editing a record, scroll to the Custom Fields section at the bottom of the form. Enter the value and save as usual.

## Showing custom fields as columns

In any module list, use the **Columns** button to toggle which fields are visible in the table. Custom fields appear alongside standard fields in that menu.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Custom fields are configured per module — so a custom field added under
  Customers only appears on Customer records, not on Orders or Products.
</Callout>
