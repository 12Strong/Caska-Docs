Caska has an iOS and Android mobile app that gives you access to key information and actions when you're away from your desk.

## Getting the app

The Caska mobile app is available on the **Apple App Store** and the **Google Play Store**. Search for "Caska" or use the link provided in your account welcome email.

## Accessing your account

Sign in with the same email and password you use on the web app. If you use Google sign-in, you'll need to create a mobile apppassword by clicking on "forgot password and setting one up.

## What's web-only for now

The following modules are available on the web only and will be added to the mobile app in future updates:

- Reports
- Settings

## Keeping the app updated

Keep your app up to date via the App Stores to get the latest features and fixes.

## Signing out

Go to the navigation drawer and tap **Sign Out**.
