Caska has an iOS mobile app that gives you access to key information and actions when you're away from your desk.

## Getting the app

The Caska mobile app is available on the **Apple App Store**. Search for "Caska" or use the link provided in your account welcome email.

Sign in with the same email and password you use on the web app. If you use Google sign-in, tap **Sign in with Google** on the login screen.

## What's web-only for now

The following modules are available on the web only and will be added to the mobile app in future updates:

- Products
- Inventory
- Batches
- Timesheets
- Fundraisers
- Reports
- Settings

## Keeping the app updated

Keep your app up to date via the App Store to get the latest features and fixes.

## Signing out

Go to the **Account** section in the navigation drawer and tap **Sign Out**.

<Callout kind="info">
  The Caska mobile app is currently iOS only. Android support is planned for a future release.
</Callout>
